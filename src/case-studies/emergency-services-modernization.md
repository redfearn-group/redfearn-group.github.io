## Situation

Emergency services providers needed to modernize their technology stack, but the usual failure mode in that world is a vendor showing up with a platform pitch before anyone has actually documented what's broken on the ground. Investment decisions were getting made off vendor demos instead of operational reality.

## What I Built

- **Authored two 50-page operational assessments** for emergency services providers, delivering vendor evaluation criteria, competitive analysis, and prioritized modernization roadmaps that guided real pilot and procurement decisions.
- **Ran six customer discovery engagements with operations leadership**, translating frontline pain points directly into prioritized product requirements and integration recommendations instead of starting from a vendor's feature list.
- **Presented findings in 30 executive briefings and stakeholder-facing sessions**, informing investment prioritization and vendor selection at the level where the budget decisions actually get made.
- **Facilitated cross-organizational strategy and governance sessions**, producing executable roadmaps that covered pilot, scale, and steady-state operations, not just the exciting first phase.

## The Hardest Problem

Getting operations leadership, the people who actually run emergency response, to trust that a technology assessment was grounded in their reality and not a consultant's template. That trust had to be earned in the discovery engagements themselves, by showing up with questions specific enough that it was obvious the work started from their operational pain, not from a vendor's pitch deck.

## Outcome

Two comprehensive assessments gave emergency services providers a procurement-ready path forward, grounded in documented operational needs rather than vendor promises. The roadmaps covered the full lifecycle from pilot to steady-state operations, which is the part most modernization plans skip.

## Skills Demonstrated

Operational assessment and technology strategy, customer discovery at the executive level, vendor evaluation frameworks, cross-organizational governance facilitation, public-sector modernization planning.
